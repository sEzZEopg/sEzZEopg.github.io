Notes

  • Recipe above modified for cast iron pan
  • Bag and freeze leftover sauce in pizza size portions
  • Mozzarella - freeze for 15-20 minutes before dicing it into 1/4 inch cubes. Cubing the cheese is way easier than grating it, and giving it a little chill makes things easier still.
  • Do not use fresh mozzarella or shreaded. It will sog out the crust and make a mess.
  • When reheating leftovers I find the best way is on a sheet pan, in a 300 degree oven for about ten minutes.
  • Light drizzle of honey on crust before cooking
